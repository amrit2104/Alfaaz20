Rules

1

Language

This competition is limited only to Hindi.

2

Entry Fee

There is no entry fee for this competition.

3

Original Content

The poem should be original. Plagiarism of any sort would lead to straight disqualification.

4

Word Limit

Poem should not be more than 200 words.

5

Judgement Criteria

In the 1st judgement criterion: The poems will be judged on the basis of Content, Originality, creativity of Title, overall impact (100 marks) (Judgement by experts of ‘Hindi_Panktiyaan’ ).

6

Judgement Criteria

In the 2nd judgement criterion: The points will be awarded based on number of likes and shares, 1 like = 2 points, 1 share = 5 points.

7

Judgement Criteria

Like, share and other reactions will be valid only if the participant has liked the Facebook and Instagram page of Alma Fiesta and Hindi Panktiyaan.

8

Result

The final result will be based on sum of points scored in above two criterions.

9

Check your Language

The poems should not have obscene language or any vulgar innuendos. Usage of such language will lead to disqualification.

10

Number of Submissions

A person is allowed to submit a maximum of 2 entries.

11

Who can Participate

Competition is open for all.
